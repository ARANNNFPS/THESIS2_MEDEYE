About MedEye

Mission

MedEye is committed to providing fast, accessible, and reliable medicine identification. Our goal is to bridge the knowledge gap for patients, caregivers, and health professionals through instant recognition and clear, trustworthy information.

How It Works

Using advanced deep learning and computer vision integrated into our backend Flask API, MedEye analyzes images of pills or packaging and returns essential information. The current web platform is intentionally designed as a lightweight demonstration—showing only a fraction of MedEye’s full capabilities. Unlike the mobile application, which performs three layers of validation (YOLO detection, OCR extraction, and a heuristic verification algorithm), the web version provides a simplified preview so users can test and experience a portion of the system’s power..
