About MediWeb

Mission

MediWeb is committed to providing fast, accessible, and reliable medicine identification. Our goal is to bridge the knowledge gap for patients, caregivers, and health professionals through instant recognition and clear, trustworthy information.

How It Works

Using advanced deep learning and computer vision (integrated in the backend Flask app), MediWeb will analyze images of pills or packaging and return key info. The web platform is designed for speed, clarity, and ease of connection to our growing database.
